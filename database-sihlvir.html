Sihlvir



Sihlvir is a female of an unknown species enhanced with cybernetics. She wields one of the Talismans.

Contents




History




Early Life


Currently, not much is known about Sihlvir's early life.

Testing the E5


For the purpose of testing the E5, the members of Ghold's group drew straws to see who would lend their Talisman. After drawing the short straw, Sihlvir lent her Talisman to Nikhel so that he could begin running tests.

Sihlvir argues with Ghold.

After some time, Nikhel and Ghold were discussing the potential success of the E5, as the preparations were a success. Sihlvir appeared and stated her dissatisfaction for lending her Talisman and that they had had it for too long. Ghold reminded her that she was safe as the others were on watch, but she explained that that was not what she meant. Ghold then taunted her by asking if it was Cyborg X and Shinobi that she was afraid of, but Nikhel interupted and reminded Sihlvir that they were using her Talisman as she drew the short straw. He then went on to say that their bickering brought up a good point since Ghold mentioned Cyborg X and Shinobi. Ghold then stated that while they tested the E5 he would act as the bait to draw the attention of the two heroes away.

Sihlvir wonders what happened to Ghold.

Shortly after Andrew defeated Ghold at the restaurant that Ghold decided to play the part of the bait at, Sihlvir arrived to see how things were going on his end as the E5 tests were complete. To her surprise she found Ghold lying unconsious outside the building near a whole in the wall, and she wondered if this was the result of an encounter with Cyborg X and Shinobi as she began to attempt to grab on Ghold with one of her metallic tentacles. When Andrew got her attention, she noticed that he was wearing Ghold's Talisman, and she asked him if he stole it. He tried to explain to her that he did not steal it and that it appeared on him, but she angrily questioned his response as she threw off her cloak and ran toward him with electricity running through her body. She declared that she would take it back from his theiving corpse, but Ghold woke up and told her to wait as he claimed that Andrew was his and a strange dark energy flowed around his body. Sihlvir smiled and Andrew looked nervous, but as Ghold noticed Cyborg X and Shinobi on the rooftop of the restaurant, he deactivated his technique. He told Sihlvir to forget it as they were done there, to which Sihlvir confusedly asked why and began to ask about the Talisman, but Ghold interupted her saying for them to go. Sihlvir angrily disagreed with Ghold's command as she told him to forget that idea, but in her attempt to persuade Ghold to stay and fight, he yelled at her, stating that it was an order. Sihlvir jumped to Ghold's side as he told Andrew that he would reclaim the Talisman that he had stolen from him, and that when that time came Andrew would die. Right then, Sihlvir retreated with Ghold in another bolt of lightning.

Personality




Sihlvir is short-tempered and confrontational in nature. Although she obeys the orders of her superior, Ghold, she does not hesitate to speak her mind or state her dissatisfaction with one of his orders if she does not agree with it.

Appearance




Sihlvir's appearance

Sihlvir is a tall female of an unknown species with cybernetic enhancements. Her skin is pink, and the horn on the top left of her head is a paler pink. The right side of her head has a metallic plating and the horn is metallic. Her ears and the sides of her head are also metallic. She has silver shoulder pads attached to a silver metallic torso. Her left arm goes down to a silver gauntlet with a red symbol on it. It contains a metallic tentacle, and her right arm is the same, although the upper part of that arm is small and pipe-like and protrudes from a cone-shaped robotic shoulder. The bottom part of her torso is a black cylinder attached to silver metallic hips with a red marking. Her upper legs are black robotics, and her lower legs and feet are silver. She also has a long mettalic tail. On her back are two small tanks of water. When Sihlvir first appeared at the restaurant, she wore a brown cloak that covered most of her body including a hood that covered most of her head. Holding the cloak closed is a single circular gold medal with a red symbol on it.

Abilities




Sihlvir uses the metallic tentacles that come from her gauntlet-like forearms. She can charge them and herself with electricity. She can also use her Lightning Jump ability to jump to a new location at super speed. Other than that, the rest of her abilities are unknown.

Romance




Sihlvir does not have any romantic interests.
