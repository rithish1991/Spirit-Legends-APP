Andrew



Andrew is a teenage boy in high school who becomes the successor to one of the Talismans.

Contents




History




Early Life


Currently, not much is known about Andrew's life as a child. He was, however, raised by his uncle as an only child.

Obtaining the Talisman


During a pep rally at his school, Andrew was sitting on the bleachers looking up at the sky feeling bored, when he suddenly saw his unrequited love, Amy, run onto the field. His admiration quickly turned into despair, however, as he witnessed her leap into the arms of her boyfriend, the captain of the school's football team, serving as a harsh reminder of the unfortunate news that Andrew already knew - that Amy had a boyfriend. Just then, his best friend, Matt, came by and apologized for being late. Noticing that Andrew was still upset, he reminded his friend that they had plans to go out to lunch, since pep rallies always meant half-days at school.

Andrew reacts to Ghold's threat towards Amy.

Now at a restaurant, Andrew and Matt overheard Amy scream. It just so happened that Amy was also at the restaurant with two of her friends, Claire and Elizabeth, and the scream was an exaggerated sigh of annoyance, as a sudden lightning storm had caused her boyfriend's football game to be cancelled. At that moment, Andrew fell back into a depressed state. Matt tried to encourage his friend to finally say something to Amy, but their one sided conversation was cut short by a mysterious cloaked figure that barged into the establishment demanding to the hostess for food or else everyone there would die. With the poor girl frozen in fear, the horned menace intended to stay true to his words as he prepared to strike her down, but a crying Amy suddenly shouted for the monster to stop. With the events feeling eerily similar to a recent nightmare she experienced where many people died in front of her, Amy declared that she did not want to watch anyone die, surprising and confusing everyone. Claiming she was brave, the figure decided to grant her an explanation on why he was there before killing her, stating that a pair of heroes named Cyborg X and Shinobi have recently appeared bringing with them a surge of hope and a longing for justice and that he wanted to remind the masses that malevolence was still as mighty as ever. This seemed to be news to Andrew, as he had not heard of these two heroes, leaving Matt in disbelief of his friend's ignorance. The cyborg then extended one of his mechanical limbs over to Amy in an attempt to claim his new target as his first victim. Seeing Amy in danger, Andrew jumped up and tried attacking the monster, only for his punch to do no damage whatsoever. The creature instead brought Amy back towards him with his retractable arm and threw her into Andrew, sending the two flying into tables with a force that would surely leave anyone heavily injured. Miraculously, the strange pendant that was hanging around the being's neck had touched Andrew's hand during his desperate attack and had seemingly appeared around Andrew's neck instead.

Andrew recalls accidentally touching the Talisman.

Finding himself in a strange void of darkness, Andrew wondered if he was dead. Another mysterious figure introduced himself as a spirit sealed within the Talisman, the strange pendant now around Andrew's neck. Explaining that they were essentially in Andrew's mind where time moved slower, the spirit continued to state that he did not remember who he was or where he came from, but he did remember someone connecting with him at one point in the past as well as someone recently tapping into his power, the latter someone being the mysterious monster. He then informed Andrew that there were in fact two spirits sealed within the Talisman and that he was a spirit capable of manipulating the element of darkness. He added that the type of seal on the Talisman is one that reacts to emotions, but only for specific individuals, meaning that Andrew must have been under great distress to reach the spirit through the seal as well as being a special person. The spirit also went on to explain that the method to reach through the seal and to the spirit sealed within varies for each of the two spirits and that to reach him Andrew was actually more worried about himself than Amy at that moment when he touched the Talisman. Even though time moved slower for them, they still did not have much time, and so the spirit explained that Andrew now had enough power to stand up to the menace.

Andrew lands a damaging punch on Ghold.

As the dust cleared from the area where Andrew and Amy were thrown, Andrew stood holding Amy, the two of them unharmed. Andrew claimed that he wanted to give that punch another shot. Noticing that Andrew now had his Talisman, the monster took the same hostess from before as a hostage, telling Andrew to give him the Talisman and in return he would spare everyone. Instead, Andrew distracted the beast by quickly throwing a knife, which caught the creature off guard long enough for Andrew to jump in close for a second attempt at a punch. With his fist hitting the metallic gut of the cyborg, the horned menace flew through multiple booths before crashing through the wall and landing outside unconscious. Their victory was cut short as, even though the storm had passed, lightning struck the ground near the unconscious monster, revealing another cloaked creature. With some newfound confidence, Andrew approached this new figure, who noticed that he was wearing the Talisman that was around the other monster's neck, revealing that the two creatures were in fact allies. Before she could attempt to take it back, the unconscious monster woke up and ordered for her to stop, as he wanted to get revenge; however, while powering up a strange cloak of darkness around himself, he ultimately decided to stop as he, unbeknownst to the others, noticed the duo, Cyborg X and Shinobi, on the roof of the restaurant. The two monsters fled the scene in a bolt of lightning, but not before the injured one threatened to eventually reclaim the Talisman and kill Andrew.

Eventually, police showed up to the scene as well as a news crew, who reported on the events. With the Talisman continually reappearing around Andrew's neck, it was unable to be separated from Andrew and taken in as evidence and secured. The paramilitary organization, PALADUS, stepped in and assigned two agents, Leo Redfeld and Clara Valentino, to act as bodyguards who would watch over and protect Andrew as well as the Talisman.

Andrew gets annoyed at Leo's concern.

That night, after returning home with the two agents at his side, Andrew sat alone in his room and tried to once again reach out to the spirit inside the Talisman, calling him "Dark-Element-Spirit-of-Shadows-Guy", to which the spirit replied that "Dark Spirit" would suffice. After thanking the spirit for his help, the two decided it would be best if Andrew learned how to use the Dark Spirit's powers. After learning how to manipulate gravity, Andrew jumped for joy, resulting in a curious Leo inviting himself into Andrew's room and asking if everything was alright and who he was talking to, much to Andrew's annoyance.

The weekend passed and Andrew found that with Leo and Clara standing guard around him at all times, everyone was too scared to be anywhere near him despite his heroic actions a few days earlier. Even Matt was questioned when he tried to approach Andrew, but an annoyed Andrew claimed that Matt was with him and for them to stand down. Matt tried to cheer Andrew up by telling him to think of the situation as him having his own personal bodyguards on top of the good news of him saving everyone three days ago, including Amy.

Shinobi's Challenge


Andrew accepts Shinobi's challenge.

After school, Andrew and Matt were walking home with Leo and Clara behind them. All of a sudden, Cyborg X and Shinobi appeared claiming that they were there to test Andrew. Before Leo and Clara could interfere, Shinobi created a barrier separating himself, Cyborg X, Leo, and Clara from Andrew and Matt, prompting Leo to call for backup. Shinobi then jumped over the barrier and launched a strange energy attack from one of the two blades that he grabbed from his back. The attack knocked Andrew into the barrier, causing him to seemingly pass out. Inside his mind, Andrew called for the Dark Spirit to lend him his strength once again, to which the spirit reminded him that he did not have to ask for it. Andrew then remembered how to fuse with the spirit and use his gravity powers. While Cyborg X prevented Leo and Clara from any attempts to get to Andrew, Shinobi launched another attack at Andrew, to which Andrew reacted by using his gravity powers to push himself off the floor, push Matt out of the way, and deflect the attack, claiming that he would pass Shinobi's test with flying colors.

Shinobi made the first move, swinging his blade once again and shooting the same energy attack as before. Andrew successfully blocked the attack by once again using his gravity powers. Once the deflective power wore off, Shinobi ran up to Andrew and unleashed a punch to his face. The Dark Spirit told Andrew to concentrate, reminding him that his senses are heightened while fused with him. Using his advice, Andrew saw a kick by Shinobi and successfully blocked it with his hands, preparing for a counterattack at the Dark Spirit's behest. Andrew's punch was blocked by Shinobi; however, who followed up with a thrust of his blade that cut Andrew's cheek. Andrew pushed Shinobi back with his powers once again and touched the blood on his cheek, reassuring the Dark Spirit that he was okay when he asked. Shinobi warned Andrew that using the Gravity Push so freely while being new to such powers would take their toll on Andrew and asked if the Dark Spirit had not taught him that. Andrew began to ask Shinobi how he knew of the Dark Spirit, but the Dark Spirit told Andrew that their fusion would drain Andrew's energy fast if his body was not accustomed to it. He told him it was time to go on the offensive. Andrew was unsure that he could even touch Shinobi, so the Dark Spirit told Andrew that it was time to learn something new.

Andrew unleashes the Shadow Claw against Shinobi.

Shinobi watched as Andrew attempted to gather darkness in his right hand and jump in joy for succeeding. Grabbing the other blade on his back, Shinobi swung it, sending another energy attack towards Andrew. At the Dark Spirit's command, Andrew jumped over it and aimed to fall towards Shinobi, but Shinobi countered with another energy attack from his other blade. Andrew attempted to use his Gravity Push once more, but it did not work thanks to overusing it. He got hit by Shinobi's attack, lost his grip on the darkness gathered in his hand, and fell to the ground below. Shinobi asked him if he had enough, and the Dark Spirit asked Andrew if he could still stand, to which he responded that Shinobi was out of his league. Shinobi asked Andrew how he could expect to fight the horned cyborgs like this, adding in that he could get up or give up, but the cyborgs would not give him a choice. Feeling motivated, Andrew stood up once more and ran towards Shinobi in an attempt to punch him, but Shinobi countered with a kick, sending Andrew backwards; however, Andrew followed up with the punch once more, to which Shinobi responded by blocking it with his swords crossed. Andrew followed up by punching the crossed swords with his other hand, breaking through Shinobi's block. Shinobi fell backwards and stabbed the ground behind him with his swords in order to prop himself up for a kick to Andrew, but Andrew successfully activated his Gravity Push once more, pushing Shinobi to the ground below. While still above Shinobi, Andrew gathered darkness into his right hand once again and unleashed the Shadow Claw technique that he was planning on using earlier, but before he could strike with it, the energy dissipated and Andrew fell unconscious, falling on Shinobi below him. After Shinobi exchanged some words with Matt, Shinobi took Andrew away from the battlefield.

Personality




Andrew is overall friendly and peace-loving in nature, but is also heroic and determined when push comes to shove. Andrew dislikes confrontation, but when lives are on the line, he does all he can to muster up the confidence he needs to save the day. He is also very naive at times, whether it's lacking knowledge of current events or misprouncing foreign names, but this only proves as a testament of his innocence and not of his lack of intelligence. He would rather be playing video games than training, but he thinks that being a hero does sound like it could be fun.

Appearance




Andrew's appearance

Andrew has spiky light brown hair with some strands hanging down over his forehead, green eyes, and light colored skin. He wears a blue-green colored sweater that for some strange reason lacks cuffs and hood drawstrings. Perhaps it was custom made? He also wears blue jeans and red and white sneakers with white low cut socks.

While fused with the Dark Spirit, Andrew's hair color changes to black and the strands of hair that were hanging over his forehead now go up with the rest of his hair. Also, some of the hair in the front separates into two different directions similarly to horns. He would have the spiky tufts of hair that the Dark Spirit has behind his ears, however, he already has those naturally. Additionally, his eyes change color to black and he gains a curved, triangular marking under each eye.

Abilities




While fused with the Dark Spirit, Andrew can manipulate the element of darkness which includes gravity and shadows. Aside from being able to pull or push things away with gravitational force, the Gravity Push is a technique that uses gravity to push away what he chooses in a short radius all around himself from the center of his body, acting like an orb of protection in an instant. This move does not come without a price, though, as one who has not yet mastered these powers will drain their energy fast.

Andrew also has access to a move called Shadow Claw, which is a large gathering of darkness that forms into a claw-like appendage that is in fact in the design of the Dark Spirit's very own hand. The claw acts as both a form of attack and defense, as it is in fact a hand made of darkness and energy that can grab, scratch, block, etc. Sometimes, a bit of forearm is made for some extra reach, and where it ends, the energy tapers off behind Andrew like cloth.

Romance




Andrew has a huge crush on Amy, who he considers the girl of his dreams. Whenever he is around her, he gets nervous and shy. After he found out that she had a boyfriend, he became a bit depressed and would fall into a slump everytime he saw them together.

Trivia & Fun Facts



  • The moment when Andrew noticed his sweater around his neck.

    When Andrew fused with the Dark Spirit, he pulls his arms out of his sweater's sleeves and lifts the bottom of the sweater up to his neck, wearing it in a similar fashion to a cape. This idea came to him when he was practicing with the gravity powers and he pulled his sweater towards himself, resulting in the sweater flying into his face, in which he pulled his head through it and it sat around his neck.

  • Andrew was initially created in first drafts of the story to be Andrew DeCrescenzo, the creator of Spirit Legends himself. After many revisions of the story and its characters, Andrew became a separate entity with some similar personality traits, but kept the name as it reflected what the creator wanted in a main character, that is, to be seen as an average everyday person.
