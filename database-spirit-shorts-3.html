Spirit Shorts #3An Unexpectable Test Spectacle


←Previous strip Next strip→

This is the third strip of the Spirit Shorts comic strip series.

Contents




Summary




Shinobi states that he and Cyborg X have come to test Andrew. Andrew accepts the challenge and declares that he will pass the test with flying colors. We then see a test in the form of a batch of papers with a grade of an "F" marked on them. A frustrated Andrew asks how he was supposed to know that that was what Shinobi meant.


Trivia & Fun Facts



  • This strip was first released at the end of Spirit Legends #3.

  • Originally, the word "Spectacle" in the title was misspelled as "Spectical". This spelling error can be seen at the end of Spirit Legends #3.

  • Andrew accepting Shinobi's challenge is a parody reference to the fact that he did the same thing in the actual story, although, unlike in the actual story, Andrew accepts it directly after Shinobi states that he and Cyborg X came to test him.
