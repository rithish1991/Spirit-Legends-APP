Nikhel



Nikhel is an elderly and intelligent male of an unknown species enhanced with cybernetics.

Contents




History




Early Life


Currently, not much is known about Nikhel's early life.

Testing the E5


For the purpose of testing the E5, the members of Ghold's group drew straws to see who would lend their Talisman. After drawing the short straw, Sihlvir lent her Talisman to Nikhel so that he could begin running tests.

Nikhel and Ghold discuss the E5.

After some time, Nikhel and Ghold were discussing the potential success of the E5, as the preparations were a success. Sihlvir appeared and stated her dissatisfaction for lending her Talisman and that they had had it for too long. Ghold reminded her that she was safe as the others were on watch, but she explained that that was not what she meant. Ghold then taunted her by asking if it was Cyborg X and Shinobi that she was afraid of, but Nikhel interupted and reminded Sihlvir that they were using her Talisman as she drew the short straw. He then went on to say that their bickering brought up a good point since Ghold mentioned Cyborg X and Shinobi. Ghold then stated that while they tested the E5 he would act as the bait to draw the attention of the two heroes away.

Personality




Nikhel is calm and collected, and he tends to take the logical approach to any situation. He is also respectful of the other members of his group, especially to their leader, Ghold. He is also highly intelligent, as he is the only one outside of Ghold's occasional inputs to work on the E5.

Appearance




Nikhel is a tall male of an unknown species with cybernetic enhancements. He is only ever seen wearing a brown cloak that covers most of his body including a hood that covers most of his head. Holding the cloak closed is a single circular gold medal with a red symbol on it. From the little bit that could be seen, his skin is pink, and the top left of his head is a paler pink. The right side of his head has a metallic plating. A Talisman can also be seen around his neck, but it is currently unknown which one he has. His forearms are metallic brown gauntlets with a red symbol on it as well as three circular patterns on each. He also has a long, triangular-shaped mettalic tail.
