Andrew's Abridged Adventures #3Plenty of Fish


←Previous strip Next strip→

This is the third strip of the Andrew's Abridged Adventures comic strip series.

Contents




Summary




From Andrew's perspective, we see Amy beautifully running and laughing. We then see her jump into her boyfriend's arms as they share a kiss. A disheartened Andrew watches on and says that the girl of his dreams is in the arms of another man. Matt supportingly puts his hand on Andrew's shoulder and says not to worry as there are plenty of fish in the sea. Andrew misunderstands the saying that Matt is quoting and asks if Amy is a fish.


Trivia & Fun Facts



  • Although not yet appearing in the main comic, this non-canon strip shows a slight redesign of Amy where she wears pink sneakers instead of pink sandals. This is the first instance of this design being shown in all Spirit Legends media.
