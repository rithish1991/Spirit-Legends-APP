This is the first strip of the Andrew's Abridged Adventures comic strip series.

Contents




Summary




At Andrew's home, Andrew holds up a figure and excitedly states that it is the newest figure for his collection. As he happily continues to stare at his updated collection, Matt places his palm on his face in disbelief over what seems like a waste of money.


Trivia & Fun Facts



  • Although it is not specifically stated, this strip takes place in Andrew's room.

  • The figures that are in Andrew's collection are homages to the real life video game characters, Pac-Man, Sonic the Hedgehog, and Link. In particular, these figures are homages to Nintendo's Near-field communication (NFC) toys known as Amiibo.

  • The character on the figure that Andrew is holding in panel one is also seen on a poster in Andrew's room, which is first seen in Spirit Legends #3.
