This is the first strip of the Spirit Shorts comic strip series.

Contents




Summary




While sitting down in the restaurant, Monkey See Monkey Eat, Andrew is about to take a bite of his burger. He then notices Ghold and asks him if he would like a seat. An enraged Ghold shouts that he is not sitting anywhere.


Trivia & Fun Facts



  • This strip was first released at the end of Spirit Legends #1.

  • Ghold shouting is a parody of when he shouts that he is not sitting anywhere in the actual story.
