Spirit Shorts #4A Claw's for Concern


←Previous strip Next strip→

This is the fourth strip of the Spirit Shorts comic strip series.

Contents




Summary




Andrew falls towards Shinobi with the Shadow Claw activated. Shinobi presents a yarn ball, and, like a cat, Andrew begins playing with it. Shinobi holds up two fingers showing that he is victorious.


Trivia & Fun Facts



  • This strip was first released at the end of Spirit Legends #4.

  • Andrew using the Shadow Claw against Shinobi is a parody reference to him doing the same thing in the actual story, although, unlike in the actual story, Andrew does not pass out from overusing the powers granted by fusing with the Dark Spirit.
