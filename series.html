Spin-Off Comics


These are the various spin-off comics for Spirit Legends! Spirit Shorts is a non-canon parody comic strip series where each strip appears at the end of its subsequent numbered issue of the main series, parodying events from that issue. Andrew's Abridged Adventures is a non-canon comic strip series that features miscellaneous events from the everyday life of Spirit Legends' main character, Andrew.
