This is the second strip of the Andrew's Abridged Adventures comic strip series.

Contents




Summary




At Andrew's home, Andrew stands on his bed and declares to no one that he is the darkness in the night and the hero of justice. His foot begins to tremble as he poses one his bed and begins to declares that, thanks to his Talisman, not even gravity can defy something, but before he can finish, he falls and yells.


Trivia & Fun Facts



  • Despite being non-canon, this strip would have to take place sometime after Andrew first practices using the powers of darkness with the Dark Spirit when he first gets the idea to wear his sweater like a cape, as he is already using his sweater like a cape in this strip.
